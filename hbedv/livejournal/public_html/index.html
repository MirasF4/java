Online questionnaire - LiveJournal friends

This questionnaire is part of a scientific study entitled "Social Networking in Online Communities - The emergence of relationships in the LiveJournal community" for gaining a masters degree at the Paris Lodron University Salzburg.

The following questionnaire was designed to analyze the relationships that emerge from the Social Networking Community LiveJournal. The answering of the questions will take you 10 minutes and will provide me with valuable information for my study.

The questions are in a variety of forms and some make use of a 6-point scale, therefore, please choose the item that most appropriately expresses your opinion.

The questionnaire is completely confidential and your answers will be used for research purposes only. The results will be published at http://mb-0220309.livejournal.com/ in January 2008.

If you have any further questions don't hesitate to contact me: michaela.beham@sbg.ac.at

Thank you very much for your participation!
